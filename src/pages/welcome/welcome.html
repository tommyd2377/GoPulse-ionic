Welcome to GoPulse

GoPulse is a social news platform that was built from the ground up to be ad free and to prevent filter bubbles. The platform only indexes articles from publishers that have been carefully vetted. With GoPulse you can:

  • Get a much broader perspective on current events
  • Discover, read and share the latest news from a growing list of mainstream publishers
  • Follow other GoPulse users to get article recomendations designed to keep you informed and open minded
